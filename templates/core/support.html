Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the crop recommendations? +
Our Random Forest AI model is trained on real agronomic data for 119 crops with feature engineering, achieving strong accuracy for Indian soil and climate conditions.
Can I use Smart-Kissan for any state in India? +
Yes! We support all major Indian cities and states. The Auto Detect feature also applies regional bias — coastal, hilly, arid and tropical-wet zones get region-specific recommendations.
How is Auto Detect different from Manual? +
Auto Detect fetches live weather and estimates regional soil data from your city name. Manual mode lets you enter your own precise soil measurements for more accurate results.
How do I get my OpenWeatherMap API key? +
Visit openweathermap.org, sign up for free, go to the API Keys section and copy your key. Paste it in settings.py as OPENWEATHER_API_KEY.
What crops does Smart-Kissan support? +
We support 119 crops including cereals, pulses, oilseeds, vegetables, fruits, plantation crops, spices, flowers and medicinal plants. Visit the All Crops page to explore the full list.
