What is Soil pH?

pH measures soil acidity. Values below 6 are acidic, above 7.5 are alkaline. Most crops prefer 6.0–7.5.

💧

What is Soil Moisture?

Moisture % indicates water content. Too low causes drought stress; too high causes root rot.

⚗️

pH Quick Reference

Acidic (<6.0): Add lime to raise pH.
Neutral (6–7.5): Ideal for most crops.
Alkaline (>7.5): Add sulfur to lower pH.

📊

Moisture Quick Reference

Low (<30%): Irrigate immediately.
Optimal (30–70%): Good for growth.
High (>70%): Improve drainage.
